I'm a PhD student at The University of Tokyo under the supervision of Professor Hideki Nakayama.

I'm generally interested in computer vision and generative models, both from a theoretical and applied perspective. In particular, I've been working on/interested in the following areas:

  • Diffusion and Flow-based Models
  • Multimodal Layout Generation
  • Image Processing and Editing
  • World Models and Video Prediction

As part of a double master's degree program, I graduated from RWTH Aachen University (supervised by Professor Jens-Rainer Ohm) and Keio University (supervised by Professor Yoshimitsu Aoki).

During my internships at Rakuten Institute of Technology, Preferred Networks, LY Corporation, and Adobe Research I was able to gain valuable experience as a research scientist. I have also been collaborating with researchers from CyberAgent AI Lab on a joint research project.

I expect to finish my PhD in March 2027. I am open to job or internship opportunities.

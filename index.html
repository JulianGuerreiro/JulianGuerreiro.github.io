Julian Jorge Andrade Guerreiro

I'm currently a PhD student at The University of Tokyo and a member of the Machine Perception Group under the supervision of Professor Hideki Nakayama. My research interests include Computer Vision, Video and Image Processing as well as Image Generation and Editing.

As part of a double naster's degree program, I graduated from RWTH Aachen University (supervised by Professor Jens-Rainer Ohm) and one from Keio University (supervised by Professor Yoshimitsu Aoki). During my internships at Rakuten Institute of Technology and Preferred Networks, I was able to gain valuable experience as a research scientist. I've further had the chance to collaborate on a joint research project with researchers from CyberAgent AI Lab.

Julian Jorge Andrade Guerreiro

I'm currently a PhD student at The University of Tokyo and a member of the Machine Perception Group under the supervision of Professor Hideki Nakayama.

My research interests include Computer Vision, Video and Image Processing as well as Generative Models.

As part of a double master's degree program, I graduated from RWTH Aachen University (supervised by Professor Jens-Rainer Ohm) and Keio University (supervised by Professor Yoshimitsu Aoki).

During my internships at Rakuten Institute of Technology, Preferred Networks, and LY Corporation, I was able to gain valuable experience as a research scientist. I have also been collaborating with researchers from CyberAgent AI Lab on a joint research project.
